Join Our Team

We're a group of developers who are trying to make it easier for students to access and use Brown University data. From dining menus to shuttle routes, we provide extensive documentation and examples of the Brown APIs. Currently, we're focused on building additional APIs and opening up more university data to the Brown community.

We have a variety of technical and non-technical roles for you to fill. Join us in helping revolutionize the state of data at Brown!
